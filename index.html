💡 THE CROSS-SELL STRATEGY

Since you run both businesses, every client touchpoint is an opportunity to upsell. A landscaping customer is a natural patio/retaining wall candidate. A masonry customer likely needs landscaping around their new installation. Always mention both on every piece of marketing material and every job site conversation.

On masonry flyers → mention landscaping On Nextdoor → post combo packages On door hangers → "We do it ALL"

FACEBOOK
Your broadest reach — organic + paid local advertising
📘
Facebook Business
2 pages · organic posts · paid local ads · community groups
FREE + PAID
SETUP STEPS
  • 1
    Create two separate Business Pages — one for Masonry, one for Landscaping, to target different audiences.
  • 2
    Fill out everything: hours, service area ZIP codes, phone number, and link to your website or estimating tool.
  • 3
    Upload a professional cover photo — a great before/after shot works perfectly.
  • 4
    Enable the Reviews tab and ask your last 10 customers to leave 5-star ratings.
WHAT TO POST (ROTATE THESE)
  • 📸
    Before & after photos — these perform extremely well in home services. Post every job.
  • 🎥
    Short video walkthroughs of work in progress. People love watching transformations.
  • 📅
    Seasonal promotions: "Spring cleanup special!", "Get your patio done before summer!"
  • ⭐
    Customer testimonials and screenshots of 5-star reviews.
  • 💡
    "Did you know?" tips — e.g., "Why a retaining wall prevents erosion and protects your foundation."
💰 Facebook Ads — even $5–10/day goes far locally. Use Local Awareness ads targeting homeowners ages 30–65 within a 5–15 mile radius. Run Lead Generation ads with a simple "Get a Free Quote" form. Boost your best before/after posts for maximum reach.
FACEBOOK GROUPS STRATEGY
  • 👥
    Join local community groups and post (where allowed): "Locally owned masonry & landscaping — happy to answer questions!"
  • 🤝
    Engage genuinely before promoting. Comment helpfully on posts, build trust, then mention your services naturally.

ALIGNABLE
Business-to-business referral network — your #1 B2B tool
🤝
Alignable
Referral network for local businesses · realtors · contractors · property managers
FREE
Alignable is a B2B goldmine. Think referrals from realtors, general contractors, property managers, HOA managers, and builders — people who constantly need your services for their clients.
  • ✅
    Complete your profile 100% — include photos, service descriptions, and your service area.
  • 🏡
    Connect with realtors — they need curb appeal work before every listing. This alone can fill your calendar.
  • 🔨
    Connect with general contractors, fence companies, and irrigation specialists — ideal co-referral partners.
  • 💬
    Send personal intro messages: "Hey, I do masonry & landscaping locally — if you ever need a sub or want to refer clients, I'd love to connect."
  • ⭐
    Ask happy customers and partners to leave you Alignable Recommendations — these are powerful trust signals.
  • 📢
    Post weekly to stay visible in your local business feed. Even a quick photo update works great.
🏘️
NEXTDOOR
Your most powerful hyperlocal tool — neighbors trust neighbors
🏘️
Nextdoor
Neighborhood-level trust · organic recommendations · targeted neighborhood ads
FREE + PAID
One great Nextdoor recommendation can turn into 10 phone calls. Neighbors trust neighbors more than any ad. This platform's ROI for local contractors is unmatched when you work it consistently.
  • 🏢
    Set up a Nextdoor Business Page (free). Add photos, services, contact info, and your service area.
  • ⭐
    Ask every satisfied customer to leave you a Nextdoor recommendation — this is the single highest-ROI action on this platform.
  • 🏷️
    Post in the Local Deals section with seasonal offers and combo specials.
  • 👀
    Respond to every post where someone asks "does anyone know a good landscaper/mason?" Be first, be helpful, be genuine.
  • 📍
    Run Nextdoor Ads targeted by specific neighborhoods you want to break into. Very affordable at the neighborhood level.
🚪
DOOR HANGERS
Neighborhood blitz strategy — "your neighbor just called us"
🚪
Door Hangers
Physical neighborhood saturation · job-site radius drops · social proof marketing
LOW COST
DESIGN MUST-HAVES
  • ★
    Bold headline: "Your Neighbors Just Called Us — Now It's Your Turn!"
  • 📸
    Include a before/after photo if possible — visual proof converts.
  • ⏰
    Limited-time offer: "10% off if you call by [date]" — urgency works.
  • 📱
    QR code linking to your Facebook page or Google reviews.
  • 📞
    Your phone number BIG and bold — easy to read at a glance.
DISTRIBUTION STRATEGY
  • 🏠
    Every time you finish a job, hit 20–30 homes around that address immediately.
  • 📍
    Mention the street: "We just completed work on Oak Street!" — hyperlocal social proof.
  • 🎨
    Use a different color for masonry vs landscaping hangers to track which converts better.
  • 🖨️
    Order from VistaPrint or GotPrint for affordable bulk printing.

FLYERS
Community boards, referral partners, and high-traffic drop spots
📄
Flyers
Bulletin boards · partner locations · real estate offices · community hubs
LOW COST
WHERE TO DISTRIBUTE
  • 🔨
    Hardware stores — Home Depot, Ace Hardware bulletin boards.
  • 🌱
    Nurseries and garden centers — perfect audience match.
  • 🏘️
    HOA community boards and neighborhood clubhouses.
  • 🏠
    Real estate offices — leave a stack, introduce yourself to agents.
  • 🏛️
    Chamber of Commerce events — bring a stack every time.
FLYER DESIGN CHECKLIST
  • 🖼️
    Eye-catching before/after photo as the centerpiece — fills top half.
  • 💬
    Benefit-focused headline: "Turn Your Yard Into the Envy of the Neighborhood"
  • 📋
    Short bullet list of your top 5–6 services. Keep it scannable.
  • 📞
    Clear call to action: "Call for a FREE Estimate" with big phone number.
  • 📱
    QR code to your reviews page + tear-off tabs at the bottom with your number.
